## STRIDE Threat Register

| Threat ID | Category | Component | Disposition | Mitigation Plan |

| T-09-EOP | Elevation of Privilege | /admin/dashboard, /admin/orgs/:orgId | mitigate | Rotas permanecem sob `<AdminRoute>` (role=admin) — este plano NÃO move as rotas admin para fora do AdminRoute. RLS `is_admin()` reforça no servidor. |
